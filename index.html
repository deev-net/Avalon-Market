We opened our doors in 1955 — and we've been part of Avalon's summers ever since.

...still guided by the same family principles: know your customers, stock what they love, and treat every season like it matters. Because here, it does.

Avalon is a special place. A barrier island community defined by its unhurried pace, its salt air, and its deep loyalty to the familiar. We fit right in. Generations of families have made a stop at our store part of their summer ritual — grabbing steaks for a Sunday grill, picking up fresh corn from the Jersey shore farms, or building a deli spread for the beach.

We don't pretend to be a big-box retailer. We're your island market — approachable, well-stocked, and run by people who care. The kind of place where the person behind the butcher counter knows how you like your steaks cut.

Certified Angus Beef · USDA Choice & Prime

The Butcher Shop

A premium butcher shop dedicated to quality, craftsmanship, time-honored tradition, and hand-crafted expertise. Our Master Butchers bring the kind of skill and knowledge you simply can't find at a chain supermarket.

We utilize the highest quality Certified Angus beef — USDA Choice and Prime grades — because we believe a great steak starts long before it makes it to the grill.

There is something special about cooking and eating outside. It makes us look at food differently, allowing for a more thoughtful preparation and a deeper appreciation of what's on the plate. We think your butcher should feel the same way.

"A great steak starts long before it hits the grill."

The Delicatessen

Our delicatessen is built around one idea: source the best, and let it speak for itself. We carry high-quality deli meats, a thoughtfully curated cheese selection, and all of the accompaniments worth seeking out.

Whether you're building a beach picnic, stocking up for the week, or putting together an effortless summer spread, our deli counter delivers. Sliced to order, with the care and knowledge that comes from doing this for a very long time.

We're also proud to be connected to 29th Street Deli ↗ — a dedicated deli experience right on the island for those looking for a sandwich done right.
